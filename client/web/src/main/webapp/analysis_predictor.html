Biomarker Prediction Analysis

Access: Public

Overview

Follow this analysis
 In this analysis, we develop a reproducible workflow designed to integrate genetic and phenotypic information from a clinical cohort and evaluate the performance of various computational modeling strategies for biomarker identification and inference of mathematical models able to accurately predict the clinical phenotype based on the identified biomarkers. The workflow contains adaptable components that can be changed to evaluate different computational strategies at each stage of the workflow.
