Charles Sawyers, Howard Hughes Medical Institute

Profile

 Dr. Sawyers is investigating the signaling pathways that drive the growth of cancer cells, with an eye toward designing new treatment options for patients with chronic myeloid leukemia, prostate cancer, and glioblastoma.

Our laboratory is focused on characterizing signal transduction pathway abnormalities in various cancers with an eye toward translational implications. One example is chronic myeloid leukemia (CML) which is caused by the BCR-ABL fusion gene, expressed as a consequence of the Philadelphia chromosome translocation.
